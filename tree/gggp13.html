Wilheim Heinrich / William Heinrich

Born: May 11, 1832 in Barmen, Germany
Died: April 25, 1893 in Brockport, NY, where he had a hotel.

He served in the Civil War and was stationed in Elmira, NY. After a few months he developed a hernia and was discharged. His cause of death was stomach cancer.
