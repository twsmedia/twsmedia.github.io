Anton Kleinhans

← Back to Great-Grandparents or GGGP List

My great-grandfather Anton's first wife was Magaret Oster (1862–1893). They married on Aug 21, 1879. They had a daughter, Florence Josephine Kleinhans (1889–1979). Anton's second wife, my great-grandmother, was Anna Preis (Anna Price). They had a son Elmer George Kleinhans, my grandfather.

Anna's first husband was Victor Staudinger (1842–1892). They married on Oct 1, 1878. Their children were Rose Staudinger (1879–1906) and William Staudinger (1884–1906).
